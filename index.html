CFD Analysis of a Fighter Jet - RAFALE


• This was a project done during a part of the subject Computational Fluid Dynamics during my 6th Semester.
• Responsibilities:
  - Entire model is designrd using SolidWorks
  - Meshed in the Workbench
  - Simulated Using ANSYS Fluent.

Objective
Flow over an 3D object: Analysing the fluid flow over a 3-Dimensional object (Rafale) using Ansys Fluent and observing the shock waves
Flow Type
Compressible Flow
Mach Used
Mach 2 - Supersonic flow regime

Comments


  • Shock waves are produced on the leading edges and body of the Aircraft.

  • Shock waves influences the Aerodynamic flow of the aircraft.

  • Pressure and Temperature increases.

  • Lift can be created.

  • Drag is Introduced.

  • The geometry needsto be optimized to fulfil the expectations.
